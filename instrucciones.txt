
TRUTH TABLE GENERATOR

This program allows you to enter a logical expression
and generate your truth table.

To use the program you must take into account the following considerations:

-You must enter a well structured logical formula.

-The propositional variables can be any letter
 uppercase or lowercase alphabet, except for T and F.

-T is interpreted as the constant True and
-F is interpreted as the constant False

-You cannot enter an expression with more than 6 logical variables

-You can use parentheses to group

-The following symbology is used for the logical operators:

  Negation (NOT):     ~
  Conjunction (AND):  &
  Disjunction (OR):   |
  Implication:	      >
  Bimplication:       =
